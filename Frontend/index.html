About Energy Club

Energy Club is a dynamic platform dedicated to promoting energy conservation within the student community. Our mission revolves around creating awareness and fostering sustainable energy consumption practices. As an interdepartmental technical club at Easwari Engineering College, we are committed to leading the way towards a greener future.

Our primary goal is encapsulated in our motto: "Leading Future Together." We take pride in being one of the most active and dedicated clubs on campus. Our student-led initiatives aim to inspire young minds to embrace environmental responsibility and societal well-being.

We achieve our mission through a variety of means, including hosting informative webinars, seminars, and awareness campaigns tailored to different educational levels. Our flagship events, Negizhi and Technotsav, run for five days annually, showcasing our commitment to sustainable energy practices and innovation. Join us on this exciting journey toward a more energy-conscious and environmentally friendly future.
