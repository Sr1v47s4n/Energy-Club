Event Description

Technotsav is a flagship event conducted by the Energy Club of Easwari Engineering College. The event is sought to provide excitement along with practical expertise. It is a blend of technical and non-technical events that is conducted to create a medium, to enhance your professional skills and furnish them. This event fosters your collaborative relationships necessary for the future hereafter. This enthusiastic event flourishes credence and people skills for you to unveil your talent and relish yourself.

Rules & Regulation
It is an individual competition.
Participants must carry their own device, preferably a laptop or a mobile device.
There will be two rounds: the Qualifier round and the Final round.
The questions in the quiz will be presented in a combination of image-based and text-based formats.
Specific timings for each round and detailed rules will be provided on the day of the event.
It is important to adhere to ethical standards: any form of malpractice or plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
Violations of the rules will result in disqualification.
